Vice Chancellor's Message

"Embracing a New Journey of Excellence"

Welcome to the official website of the University of Dhaka. It is an honor for me to serve as the Vice Chancellor. Established in 1921, our university has been an integral part of Bangladesh's history, playing a critical role in shaping national aspirations from the Language Movement of 1952 to the recent July uprising of 2024.

As we stand at a crossroads, we remain committed to academic excellence, innovative research, and nation-building. We invite new generation scholars and all stakeholders to join us in this journey of positive transformation to convert this university into a global center of excellence.

Professor Niaz Ahmed Khan, PhD

Vice Chancellor, University of Dhaka

Our Mission
